What is ISTE?

ISTE is a national professional non profit making society for the technical education system in our country with the motto of career development of teachers and personality development of students and overall development of our technical education. But we at ISTE GRIET SB do not want to stop at our pre made agenda. So creativity and art are also a part of our activities as we believe that art challenges technology and technology inspires art. All in all, ISTE GRIET SB is the educational illuminati, the warden of knowledge and the pride for overall development.
